Hi there! So you want to know about me? Well, let me
get straight to the point. I started programming in 2014
due to my love and passion for video games on mobile devices.
Everything amazed me! I had so many questions, how is it
possible for ordinary controllers to control pixels and
characters on the screen? what are these plastic controllers
made of? How does my television screen turn on? How does all
the electrical gadgets connect to form a working input?
And so much more...

My passion for cybersecurity was because of the Stuxnet attack
I was so curious to know how it was possible for
a virus to damage the infrastructure of a country nuclear program.
Everything amazed me. The DEFCON CTF challenge
also made me to fall in love with the defensive team of security.

I learnt a lot due to my curiosity over the years and who I am
today is as a result of that.
